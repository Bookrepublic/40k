Smart Ebooks
for Smart People

40K is a digitally native imprint founded in 2010 and it is a sister company to Italian e-bookseller Bookrepublic. Our emancipation from paper allows us to publish and distribute books in different languages at their natural length. So we can publish novelettes, novellas and highly focused essays that deliver our readers knowledge in less than an hour of investment in terms of reading time.
